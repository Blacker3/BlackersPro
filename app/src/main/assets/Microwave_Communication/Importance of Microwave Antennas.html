Importance of Microwave
Antennas
A system used for transferring the data between persons and equipments is called
communication system. The system usually consists of
individual Communication networks, relay stations, transmission system, terminal
equipment, interconnection cable and inter-operations performing as an integrated
whole.
Antenna plays a crucial role in this communication system, which is used to transmit and
receive the data. The classification of the antenna is based on the specifications like
frequency, polarization, radiation, etc.
The antenna that is operated at microwave frequency is knows as microwave antenna.
There are different types of microwave antennas over a wide range of applications
including home- communication based applications.
Classification of microwave antennas:
Micro strip patch antenna
1. Horn antenna
2. Parabolic antenna
3. Plasma antenna
4. MIMO antenna
